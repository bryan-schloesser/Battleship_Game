Bryan's Battleship How To Play

Step 1

You will first choose 10 numbers. These will match the coordinates of where you would like to place your ships.

Step 2

Once you have your 10 numbers entered in, click the submit button. This will bring you to the gameboard to start the battle

Step 3

Click on one of the tiles to choose where the computer has placed its ships. The tile will change based on the outcome. Red is a hit, Gray is a miss.

Step 4

After your turn, the computer will guess where you placed your ships! If you hit all the computer ships before it destroys all yours... YOU WIN!
